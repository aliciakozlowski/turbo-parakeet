about me

I'm Alicia. I’m an senior Product Design student at the University of Cincinnati, College of DAAP.

For me, design is more than just the product, it’s about the person. Meeting incredible people and serving them by sharing their experiences and creating products that bring joy is why I love design. People. Experiences. Products. Joy. That’s what I’m going for.

Outside of design, my passions include baking gluten-free goodies, going on hikes or walks outside, and being the "grandma friend" crocheting and quilting gifts for those I love. I also have a small Etsy shop for wedding calligraphy that brings me a lot of joy.
